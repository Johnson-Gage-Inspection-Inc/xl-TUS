This document serves as a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for authenticating to workbooks that communicate with SharePoint and jgiapi.com, particularly the TUS workbook.

These steps should be carried out when you see this pop up:

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1fd37086-c81a-473c-bf05-2a41dfde246d)

---

# Steps

The steps are the same for both SharePoint and jgiapi.com, but must be completed for each.
Multifactor authentication (MFA) is enabled for our Azure tenant, so it's good to have your phone with you.

These are the three steps, summarized:

1. Select `Organizational Account`
2. Sign in with your work account (you@jgiquality.com)
3. Click `Connect`

Simply repeat these for both jgiapi.com and SharePoint.
